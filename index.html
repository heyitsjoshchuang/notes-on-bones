Welcome!

This site is a compilation of resources that I find useful as an self-taught bones player, a place where I share my thoughts on bones, as well as a personal wiki, where I organize what I’ve learnt into chapters by difficulty. All of them are created with an aim to guide those with the same interest in bones along the way. If that’s you, welcome :) and I hope we can have fun learning together.

The resource compilation is organized into a single post: Resource Compilation

What are bones?

The bones, often rhythm bones or Irish bones, to avoid ambiguation, is a percussion instrument. It consists of two sticks that are held in a single hand, and you can strike them together using various movements to create complex rhythms.
